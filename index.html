Available Classes

Yoga

Welcome to our yoga class! In this class, we will focus on building strength, flexibility, and mindfulness through a series of yoga poses and breathing exercises. We will start with a brief centering exercise to bring our focus inward and set our intentions for the practice.

English

Welcome to our English class! In this class, we will focus on improving our language skills, including reading, writing, speaking, and listening. We will start by exploring different literary genres, such as fiction, non-fiction, poetry, and drama. We will analyze the elements of these genres, including plot, character, setting, and theme.

Mathematics

Welcome to our mathematics class! In this class, we will focus on developing our problem-solving and critical thinking skills through the study of mathematical concepts and their applications. Throughout the class, we will work on solving problems and applying mathematical concepts to real-world situations.
